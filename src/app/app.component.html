SWAP - A new age barter.


If you are looking for exchanging a product that you own with the one you desire to have, then this is MUST HAVE App on your mobile. The App brings an incomparable, unique experience to swap anything that you own with literally anything that you can think of. The App may also save you from selling the product that you loved at throw-away prices in other online platforms.

It's beautiful

In the age of a consumer having multiple products with a limited utility period, we’re bringing back the concept of exchanging, renting out & donating pre-owned products & services.

Exchange products & services with another without spending money.

Easy to Use

Take pictures of all your items that you like to swap & include them to your account by clicking Save Product/Service

Post Ad by including the added stuff that you want to swap or exchange with the products or services you wish for.

Once someone likes your Ad & offer his/her product or service then you'll see that in Offers Received Accept/Reject based on your wish and close the deal by contacting the other swapper with details provided.
